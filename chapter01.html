Chapter1

The red woman

Baker street was quite and the snow was falling when Holmes arrived waking me up in an upsetting way. I suppose after all these years I am not yet used to his ways. He looked at me and said:
-Dress up, we have an important appointment.
10 minutes had passed when a beautiful woman crossed the door with 2 servants and a maid. She was dressed in red, bright and rich. The dress was made of fabrics I had never seen before. Anyone could see that she was very rich and not the type of person to come to 112 Baker Street. So that must have been a very interesting case for Holmes.
